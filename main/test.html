    What Is work visa?

    China Work Visa (also called Z visa or work residence permit) is issued to an alien who comes to China for a post or employment. Company shareholders and legal representatives are eligible to obtain working visas.

    Business visa

    What is Business visa ?

    When you get an M visa issued from the Chinese Embassy/Consulate in any other countries, normally the visa is in light green color, on the right top of the visa is the National Emblem of China. After you get your visa, you must first check if your name, passport number, birth date are correct or not.


    • Category: M (before 1st Sept 2013, we called F visa)
    • Entries: M (Multiple)
    • Enter Before: 3rd Sept 2014
    • Duration of each stay: 030 days after entry
    • Issue date: 3rd Sept 2013

    It means, your current visa type is M visa (China business visa), you have Multi-entries to enter China (you can enter / leave / re-enter China as many times as you want), But you must enter China before 3rd Sept 2014. After you enter China, you can stay maximum 30 days without leaving (30 days calculated from your last entry date into China).


    1.Processing time is short (7-10 business days)
    2. No requirements for education and work experience

    Permanent Residence

    What is green card ?

    A green card is a permanent residence permit for foreign nationals. Holding a green card means that the cardholder has the right of abode in the issuing country and, at the same time, the holder of the green card can waive the entry visa for a certain period of time.

    Can enjoy the same treatment as Chinese citizens. It's for 10 years continuous stay, and multiple entries.If you got the China green card.
